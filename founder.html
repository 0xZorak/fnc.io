Five Nines Consulting

At Five Nines Consulting (FNC), we've always been committed to precision, reliability, and delivering high-quality service. We understand that businesses require dependable support at all times, and we’re stepping up to ensure you get exactly that.

What this means for you
  • 24/7 Coverage, No matter the time, our team is ready to assist.
  • Weekend & Night Shift Availability, Business doesn't stop, and neither do we.
  • Dedicated Vendor Option, We can be your sole IT & consulting provider, ensuring seamless integration and dedicated service.
  • Emergency Response, If you require immediate attention at odd hours, you will be covered.
What this means for our team & pricing
  • Premium Compensation for Employees. Those working weekends, night shifts, and emergency calls will receive additional pay.
  • Customer Cost Adjustments. Extended-hour support, overnight responses, and urgent requests will reflect in premium pricing for sustainability.
  • Time Zone Adaptation. As we transition into full coverage, we ask for patience while we align our team across different global time zones.
