Blockchain MFA

FNC's BLockchain MFA a decentralized, blockchain-powered MFA ecosystem, integrating World Mobile Token (WTMX) for authentication and Cardano-based NFTs for subscription-based access control. Customers will stake Five Nines Consulting Token (FNC) to gain and retain access, distributing staking rewards to service providers for a self-sustaining authentication infrastructure.

By integrating Cardano smart contracts, NFTs, and WTMX-powered transactions, this system ensures cost efficiency, decentralization, and tamper-proof authentication.
