Why Join FNC?

At FNC, we don’t just talk about impact—we engineer it. Through hands-on work, world-class training, and practical support systems, we empower veterans, civilians, and volunteers alike to take control of their futures. You can join us to:

Learn new digital skills for free

Contribute as a technical volunteer, trainer, or advisor

Be part of a veteran-built infrastructure revolution

Connect your company with skilled, disciplined labor

Who We Serve

  1. U.S. military veterans and transitioning service members
  2. Remote learners and jobseekers with limited access to opportunity
  3. Mission-aligned professionals, volunteers, and mentors
  4. Organizations looking to partner with a purpose-driven workforce

We’re Currently Accepting:

  1. Veterans interested in housing, training, and entrepreneurship

Eligibility

To join our training or enterprise programs, you must:

  • Be a U.S. military veteran OR a mission-aligned learner
  • Be committed to completing assigned training or task cycles
  • Have basic access to a phone or computer
