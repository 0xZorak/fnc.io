Founder’s Story

Vasiliy S. Ivanov Vasiliy S. Ivanov is a technology strategist, military veteran, and the driving force behind Five Nines Consulting (FNC). With a career spanning advanced IT, cybersecurity, and blockchain solutions, Vasiliy has built a reputation for innovation, resilience, and leadership in high-stakes environments.

A Foundation of Excellence
Born into a Russian-Ukrainian family, Vasiliy exhibited exceptional analytical abilities from an early age, consistently ranking in the 99th percentile in mathematics and computer science. His deep fascination with physics led him to study it extensively, honing a problem-solving mindset that seamlessly translates into his work in IT and business strategy. This intellectual curiosity fuels his long-term ambition to pursue a Ph.D. in physics, applying scientific principles to drive future technological advancements.
