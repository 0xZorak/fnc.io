Founder’s Story

Vasiliy S. Ivanov Vasiliy S. Ivanov is a technology strategist, military veteran, and the driving force behind Five Nines Consulting (FNC). With a career spanning advanced IT, cybersecurity, and blockchain solutions, Vasiliy has built a reputation for innovation, resilience, and leadership in high-stakes environments.

A Foundation of Excellence
Born into a Russian-Ukrainian family, Vasiliy exhibited exceptional analytical abilities from an early age, consistently ranking in the 99th percentile in mathematics and computer science. His deep fascination with physics led him to study it extensively, honing a problem-solving mindset that seamlessly translates into his work in IT and business strategy. This intellectual curiosity fuels his long-term ambition to pursue a Ph.D. in physics, applying scientific principles to drive future technological advancements.

Military Service & Technical Mastery

Vasiliy enlisted in the U.S. Army as an IT specialist, quickly rising to prominence within the 82nd Airborne Division. He became a go-to expert for mission-critical IT operations, network security, and tactical infrastructure deployment. Deployed to Afghanistan, Vasiliy took on responsibilities far beyond his rank, ensuring seamless technical operations in high-pressure environments.

His ability to break down complex IT problems into step-by-step solutions allowed even non-technical personnel to execute high-level troubleshooting—an approach that would later define his leadership style.

After transitioning from military service, Vasiliy leveraged his expertise to found Five Nines Consulting, an IT and blockchain consulting firm with a mission to empower veterans through technology.

A Vision for the Future

Vasiliy’s vision for FNC extends beyond consulting—he aims to redefine veteran integration into the tech industry. By creating structured mentorship programs and high-impact technical solutions, he ensures that veterans are not just given jobs but equipped with the skills to lead in cutting-edge fields.

Despite facing health challenges due to burn pit exposure in Afghanistan, Vasiliy remains committed to innovation and leadership. His ability to adapt, automate, and problem-solve continues to drive FNC forward, attracting top-tier talent and forward-thinking investors who believe in the firm’s mission

With a relentless commitment to efficiency, excellence, and veteran empowerment, Vasiliy S. Ivanov is not just leading a company—he’s building a legacy.

At Five Nines Consulting, we’re not just solving today’s IT problems—we’re shaping the future. Connect with us to learn how you can be part of this journey.
